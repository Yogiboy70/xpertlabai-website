The Founder

25 years of lived experience, embedded in every product.

Yogesh spent 25 years building and leading executive search and RPO operations across some of India's most respected firms — ABC Consultants, Randstad and Korn Ferry. He's placed thousands of candidates, managed hundreds of client relationships and lived inside the spreadsheets, WhatsApp threads and legacy systems that mid-size Indian businesses rely on.

In 2026, after two years of deep immersion in AI — building expertise, testing tools, and developing products from the ground up — he launched Xpert Lab AI with a simple premise: the tools that Indian mid-size firms need don't exist yet. Global software is built for global workflows. Indian businesses — relationship-driven, approval-heavy, privacy-conscious and deeply domain-specific — deserve software built for how they actually operate.

Every product Xpert Lab AI ships is built with that context embedded. Not as an afterthought. As the starting point.

What We Believe

Three beliefs. Every product.
No exceptions.

💡

Software should earn its place.

We don't build features for the sake of a pricing tier. Every function in every product exists because a real practitioner in a real firm needed it. If it doesn't solve a problem, it doesn't ship.

🤖

AI should be invisible.

The best AI integration is the one your team uses without thinking about it — because it's woven into the workflow, not bolted on as a separate module with its own login and learning curve.

🔒

You should own your data.

Your client relationships, candidate database, pipeline, financials — these are your most valuable commercial assets. You should own them. Fully. Always. That's why Closr deploys into your cloud, not ours.

The Company

Lean by design. Disciplined by choice.

Xpert Lab AI is currently a founder-led operation — building, shipping and supporting every product directly. The goal is five paying clients before the first hire. We're not scaling headcount ahead of value delivered.

That discipline keeps the software honest. Every feature exists because a live client needed it. Every product has been tested in real deployments with real data. When you work with Xpert Lab AI, you're working with the person who built it.
