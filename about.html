The Founder

Domain expertise meets state-of-the-art AI — built from the ground up.

Xpert Lab AI was founded by Yogesh Saigal — 25 years across executive search and RPO at some of India's most respected firms. That experience didn't just inform the products. It drove two years of obsessive, often frustrating ground-up development — ideating, failing, rebuilding and solving problems that no off-the-shelf tool was addressing.

The first two years were spent in stealth — running real pilots with leading Indian organisations across manufacturing, consulting and executive search, learning what actually works versus what looks good in a demo. The result is software that has been tested against real data, real users and real resistance before a single rupee of marketing was spent.

What makes Xpert Lab AI different is not just the domain knowledge — it's the access to top AI and software professionals across geographies who ensure every product reflects the current state of the art. These are not wrappers around existing tools. Every core problem has been ideated from scratch and solved from the ground up.

What We Believe

Three beliefs. Every product.
No exceptions.

💡

Software should earn its place.

We don't build features for the sake of a pricing tier. Every function in every product exists because a real practitioner in a real firm needed it. If it doesn't solve a problem, it doesn't ship.

🤖

AI should be invisible.

The best AI integration is the one your team uses without thinking about it — because it's woven into the workflow, not bolted on as a separate module with its own login and learning curve.

🔒

You should own your data.

Your client relationships, candidate database, pipeline, financials — these are your most valuable commercial assets. You should own them. Fully. Always. That's why Closr deploys into your cloud, not ours.

The Company

Lean team. Deep expertise. State-of-the-art technology.

Xpert Lab AI operates as a lean, focused team — combining deep domain expertise with specialist technology talent drawn from across geographies. Every product is built, deployed and supported by people who understand the problem first-hand.

We work on a simple principle: build only what solves a real problem, test it with real clients, and stay close to the work. Every feature in every product exists because a live client needed it.
