The Problem

Recruitment teams are drowning in admin. Quality is suffering because of it.

Your recruiters spend 60–70% of their time on tasks that don't require human judgment — screening CVs, updating spreadsheets, chasing status, formatting shortlists, sending follow-ups. The work that actually matters — assessing fit, building relationships, understanding what a client really needs — gets squeezed into whatever's left.

The result: slower turnaround, inconsistent quality, rising costs, and team burnout. Xpert ATS is built to reverse that equation — automate the routine, amplify the judgment, and let your team of five deliver like fifteen.

Features

Every feature built around how
Indian recruiters actually work.

🤖

AI Classification Engine

Upload a CV and Xpert ATS instantly reads, scores and places it into the right talent tier — with confidence scores and AI reasoning your team can interrogate and trust.

🔍

Natural Language Search

Search your entire candidate database in plain English. "Operations head, 12 years manufacturing, Pune or Mumbai." Ranked results in seconds, not hours.

📊

Recruitment Analytics

Time-to-fill, source quality, offer-to-join ratios, recruiter performance. Real data on what's working and what isn't — so every hiring decision gets smarter over time.

📋

Full Pipeline Visibility

From first contact to offer accepted — every stage, every recruiter's activity, every candidate's status, in real time. No more chasing updates on WhatsApp or email threads.

🔬

Candidate Research Automation

Auto-enrich profiles with publicly available data. Generate structured candidate summaries ready for client presentation. Cut research time by 70% without cutting quality.

💼

Jobs Portal & Auto-Matching

Post roles, auto-match candidates from your database, score fit against the JD, and export client-ready shortlists — all within Xpert ATS, no manual comparison required.

⚡

Workflow Automation

Automated screening emails, interview scheduling, follow-up reminders, stage-move notifications. Your team stays focused on the work that requires human judgment.

🎯

Quality of Hire Tracking

Track post-placement outcomes. Link candidate quality scores back to hiring decisions. Build a proprietary intelligence layer on what predicts success in each role type.
