The Problem

Recruitment teams are drowning in admin. Quality is suffering because of it.

Your recruiters spend 60–70% of their time on tasks that don't require human judgment — screening CVs, updating spreadsheets, chasing status, formatting shortlists, sending follow-ups. The work that actually matters — assessing fit, building relationships, understanding what a client really needs — gets squeezed into whatever's left.

The result: slower turnaround, inconsistent quality, rising costs, and team burnout. Xpert ATS is built to reverse that equation — automate the routine, amplify the judgment, and let your team of five deliver like fifteen.

AI Classification Engine
