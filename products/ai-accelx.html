The Challenge

Your leadership has approved "AI adoption." Now what?

The problem isn't ambition. It's structure. Most organisations send one person to an AI workshop, buy a ChatGPT subscription, and declare transformation. Six months later, the spreadsheets are still there and nothing has changed.

Real AI transformation requires a methodology — a sequenced programme that builds capability, identifies use cases, pilots real tools and embeds new ways of working permanently. That's what AI AccelX delivers.

14 weeks. Four phases. Permanent change.

The Programme

14 weeks. Four phases.
Permanent change.

01
Weeks 1–3

Foundation

AI literacy across your leadership and operations teams. Understanding what AI can and cannot do. Mapping your current processes to identify transformation opportunities. Building the business case for change.

02
Weeks 4–7

Discovery & Design

Deep-dive process audits across your key functions. Identifying the highest-value AI use cases specific to your organisation. Designing the future-state workflows. Selecting the right tools and platforms for your context.

03
Weeks 8–11

Build & Pilot

Deploying AI tools against your real processes and real data. Piloting with live teams. Iterating based on actual usage. Building internal champions who can sustain and extend the transformation.

04
Weeks 12–14

Scale & Institutionalise

Establishing your AI-driven Shared Service Centre. Standing up the Centre of Excellence. Governance, documentation and the internal capability to keep evolving — without depending on external consultants forever.
