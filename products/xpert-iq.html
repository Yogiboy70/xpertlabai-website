How It Works

From file to intelligence
in three steps.

1

Upload

Drag and drop your invoice file — Excel, CSV or PDF. Xpert IQ auto-detects your column structure and normalises the data. No template required.

2

Explore

Your dashboard builds instantly. Revenue, Cost, Gross Profit, Margin. Client leaderboards. Segment breakdowns. Consultant performance. Monthly, Quarterly, Annual views.

3

Ask

Open the AI Assistant and ask in plain English: "Which segment had the highest margin in Q3?" Claude answers from your actual data — not a generic response.
