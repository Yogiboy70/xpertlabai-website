How It Works

From file to intelligence
in three steps.

1

Upload

Drag and drop your invoice file — Excel, CSV or PDF. Xpert IQ auto-detects your column structure and normalises the data. No template required.

2

Explore

Your dashboard builds instantly. Revenue, Cost, Gross Profit, Margin. Client leaderboards. Segment breakdowns. Monthly, Quarterly, Annual views — live.

3

Ask

Open the AI Assistant and ask in plain English: "Which segment had the highest margin in Q3?" Get a precise, context-aware answer — built on your actual numbers.

Features

Everything your finance team needs.
Nothing they don't.

📈

Revenue & Cost Analysis

Full breakdown of Revenue, Cost, Gross Profit and Margin — by client, segment, consultant and time period. Instantly filterable, always current.

🏆

Client Leaderboard

Ranked view of your top clients by revenue, margin and activity. Know exactly who drives your business and which relationships need attention.

📅

Period Intelligence

Toggle between Monthly, Quarterly and Annual views. Switch between financial years. Charts and KPIs re-aggregate in real time without manual rework.

🔎

Transaction Explorer

Search, filter and sort every invoice record. Save filter combinations as named views. Export any filtered dataset to Excel in one click.

🤖

AI Financial Assistant

Ask any question about your data in plain English and get a precise, context-aware answer. "What's my receivables exposure this quarter?" Answered in seconds.

Multi-Year Comparison

Anomaly Detection
