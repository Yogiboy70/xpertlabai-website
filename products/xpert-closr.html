The Problem

Most CRMs are databases. Yours should be a discipline engine.

Your team logs deals. But deals close without reasons. Clients go cold without alerts. New contacts get added without checks. At the end of the quarter, no one can explain why three opportunities disappeared from the pipeline. Global CRMs weren't built for the way Indian B2B teams work — relationship-led, approval-driven and deeply privacy-conscious. Xpert Closr was.

Features

Six features. One pipeline
that runs itself.

✅

Client Approval Workflow

Every new client enters through a structured gate — submitted, reviewed, approved or rejected with a recorded reason. Duplicates flagged automatically. Your pipeline stays clean from day one.

⚖️

Weighted Pipeline

Every stage carries a probability. Lead at 10%. Proposal at 75%. Won at 100%. Your forecast shows what your pipeline is actually worth — not what your most optimistic advisor said last Friday.

📋

Activity Tracking

Log calls, meetings, emails, WhatsApp conversations and notes against every client. Capture duration, outcome and follow-up date. The full relationship history, always one click away.

📊

Activity Intelligence Dashboard

Daily, weekly and monthly views. Advisor leaderboard. Activity by type. Automatic stale client flagging — any client with no contact in 7+ days is surfaced immediately. No client goes cold unnoticed.

🎯

Mandatory Win/Loss Capture

Every deal that closes — won or lost — requires a reason before it moves. Over time this builds a proprietary intelligence layer on why you win and why you lose. Most firms never have this data. Yours will.

✔️

Task Management

Create tasks linked to clients, assign to advisors, set priority and due dates. Overdue tasks flagged automatically. Nothing gets forgotten because no one checked their email.
