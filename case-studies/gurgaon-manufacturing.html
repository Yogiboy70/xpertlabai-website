The challenge

Hiring across plants and functions with no central thread.

For a pan-India manufacturing operation of this scale, recruitment never stops. Plants in multiple locations hiring simultaneously — floor supervisors, engineers, quality managers, logistics heads — each site running its own process, its own spreadsheets, its own version of what was open and what had already been filled.

The TA team at HQ in Gurgaon had no single view of what was happening across locations. Roles were being worked on in parallel without coordination. Candidates were sometimes approached twice. Plant managers didn't know where things stood without calling someone. It wasn't a people problem — it was a visibility problem that no spreadsheet network was ever going to fix.

The solution

Xpert ATS: one pipeline across every plant, every role.

Xpert ATS brought all open roles — across all locations and functions — into a single consolidated pipeline. Every plant visible. Every stage tracked. The Gurgaon TA team and on-site hiring managers working from the exact same picture for the first time.

Duplicate effort across locations disappeared. The TA team stopped spending time reconciling information across plants and started spending it on what actually matters: finding and moving the right people through the process, wherever they were needed.

Plant managers could see the status of every open role without calling HQ. Leadership could see, at a glance, where the entire organisation stood on its hiring targets — across every site, in one view.

Early deployment — and already a strong foundation. The results here represent the first phase; we're only getting started.
The results

50% more effective. From the very first month.

Within the first phase of deployment, the TA team measured a 50% improvement in overall effectiveness — roles progressing faster across all locations, fewer candidates lost between plants, and a clear audit trail for every hiring decision at every site.

The single consolidated view didn't just save time. It changed how the team worked — from reactive and fragmented across geography to coordinated and proactive from a single HQ view. That shift is the foundation everything else gets built on.

Hiring chaos is fixable.
It starts with visibility.

If your TA team is working across spreadsheets and group chats, Xpert ATS can change that in weeks — not months.
