The challenge

Hiring across functions with no central thread.

For a plant operation of this scale, recruitment is continuous. Floor supervisors, engineers, quality managers, logistics heads — multiple functions hiring simultaneously, each with their own spreadsheets, their own tracking, their own version of what was open and what had already been filled.

The TA team had no single view of what was happening. Roles were being worked on in parallel without coordination. Candidates were sometimes approached twice. Managers didn't know where things stood without chasing someone by phone or message. It wasn't a people problem — it was a visibility problem that no spreadsheet was ever going to fix.

The solution

Xpert ATS: one pipeline for every open role in the plant.

Xpert ATS brought all open roles — across all functions — into a single consolidated pipeline. Every stage visible. Every candidate tracked. Every team member working from the same picture.

Duplicate effort disappeared. The TA team stopped spending time reconciling information across departments and started spending it on what actually matters: finding and moving the right people through the process.

For the first time, hiring managers could see the status of their roles without asking anyone. And leadership could see, at a glance, where the plant stood on its hiring targets.

Early deployment — and already a strong foundation. The results here represent the first phase; we're only getting started.
The results

50% more effective. From the very first month.

Within the first phase of deployment, the TA team measured a 50% improvement in overall effectiveness — roles progressing faster, fewer candidates lost in the process, and a clear audit trail for every hiring decision.

The single consolidated pipeline didn't just save time. It changed how the team worked — from reactive and fragmented to coordinated and proactive. That shift is the foundation everything else gets built on.

Hiring chaos is fixable.
It starts with visibility.
